Something about me

A war veteran, former missionary, and married with children.
Angelic troublemaker, skeptical optimist, ambivert.
Amateur self portrait photographer.

At seventeen years old, after watching Top Gun, I left home to get a military education and serve in the Navy (four years of service including six months in the Gulf War).

In my fantasy journey to become Batman, I almost killed myself while climbing a glacier in search of the League of Shadows.

I backpack solo in the wilderness because it's cool, for the pictures, and to be an awesome dad.

I served fifteen months as a missionary, and as of today, I survived from pancreatic cancer.
