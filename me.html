Gianni J. Favaretto

Angelic troublemaker, skeptical optimist, ambivert.

At seventeen years old, after watching Top Gun, I left home to serve four years in the Navy (including participating in the Gulf War). In my fantasy journey to become Batman, I almost killed himself while climbing a glacier in search of the League of Shadows.
I backpack solo in the wilderness because I think it's cool, for the pictures, and to be an awesome dad. I lead my colleagues to climbing adventures. I served fifteen months as a missionary, and, for now, I survived from pancreatic cancer.
