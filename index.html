Oct 2019 - Jul 2020

Head of Experience Design

@ Zopa Bank (UK)

I led the Experience Design team (Research, Product and Service Design) that works with engineering and product to create user-centred financial experiences across all digital channels.

Jan - Oct 2019

Head of UX and Product Design

@ Sainsbury's Argos (UK)

I led the User Experience, Product Design, UX Research, and UX Writing teams to create digital experiences across mobile, desktop, application, in-store and new channels as they emerge in Argos, Argos Financial Services, Tu Clothing, and Habitat.

Mar 2012 - Jan 2019

UX and Design Chapter Lead

@ Ocado Technology (UK, PL, ES)

From a startup to a scale-up phase, I built, scaled and led the UX and Design team while crafting and directing the user experience and design outcomes of the Ocado Smart Platform (B2B and B2C), Fabled by Marie Claire, Fetch (the pet store) and Sizzle.co.uk.

Mar 2010 - Sept 2012

UX Manager and Sr Designer

@ Ocado (UK)

I crafted and led the design of the core UX features of the Webshop and awarded IOS native mobile and tablet apps. I have been on the front-line in delivering Ocado's most significant shopping experience redesign.

2002 - 2010

Designer, Consultant, Instructor

@ Freelance (Italy, USA, UAE)

I being providing to my international clientele several services such as User Experience, Design, Art Direction, UI Development, Accessibility consultancy, Branding and Training.

Clients included Sun Microsystem, Nasa, ENI, Novartis, Thomson Reuters, Sacbo International Airport, Fila, CIM e-Banking, Bergamo Council, and more.

2002 - 2005

Consultant, Speaker, and Instructor

@ Macromedia (Italy)

I was elected to be part of the Macromedia Specialist Group (20/25 over 600 professionals). Consultant, instructor and speaker during roadshows and conferences held by Macromedia (acquired by Adobe in 2005).

2001 - 2002

Web Art Director

@ Sesaab Group (Italy)

I headed up a team of web designers. I Designed and supervised the delivery of user interfaces and all the graphics that enhanced the appearance of websites and web applications. I educated clients about the similarities and differences between Internet communication and other forms of marketing and public relations efforts.

2000 - 2001

Web Designer

@ ANS (USA)

As a web designer in a start-up enviroment, I translated the client vision into a useable and visually appealing web solution. I worked side by side with a team of high profile developers and network specialist.

1999 - 2001

Web Designer and Prof Assistant

@ Utah Valley University (USA)

I worked with Professor Gale on evaluating students' web design work. I designed and developed the University’s websites and educational applications based on W3C accessibility standards.

I was born in the countryside of Sussex (UK). Yet, I ended up growing up – with my other seven siblings – amid the Renaissance's art and the green of Tuscany (Italy).

At the age of seventeen, I joined and served the Navy for four years; from graduating from the Academy to serving six months in the Gulf War as Radar Lead Sergeant.

I served as an LDS missionary for fifteen months instead of the standard twenty-four. Why? Despite being the most successful missionary in the field (the only one to earn the Master Teacher title), I was seen as too unconventional.

After a couple of years of self-taught web design and programming, I left my employment and all material possessions to seek Computer Science education in the United States. All done with a six months old child and an adventurous wife.

To build teams of influential individuals and team players, I organise and guide my colleagues into outdoor adventures. Adventures such as climbing to the peaks of the Dolomites and French Alps and backpacking across vulcanos and glaciers in Iceland undoubtedly boosted confidence, resilience, courage, problem-solving, and leadership skills.

To stay insane but physically and mentally healthy, I run regularly, hike, climb, and – at least once a year – backpack solo in the wilderness.

Against all the odds, I'm [still] married and a father of two.
